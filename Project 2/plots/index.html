We know CA is the state with the highest number of accidents. Now we want to determine what hour during the day do accidents happen the most. To demonstrate this, we transformed all our "Start_Time" to military time - hours are numbered from 00 to 23. Using a seaborn countplot, we can see most accidents happen around at 8 in nope morning and again at 5 in nope afternoon. Usually the "rush hour" times.
