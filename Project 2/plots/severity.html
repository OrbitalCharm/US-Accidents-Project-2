Level of Severity of Accidents in CA The severity of the accident is measured between 1 and 4, where 1 indicates the least impact on traffic (i.e., short delay as a result of the accident) and 4 indicates a significant impact on traffic (i.e., long delay). In 2019 for CA, Level 2 accidents report were the highest in CA for a total of 168,550. 42,791 were Level 3, 1,978 for Level 3, and only 18 Level 1 accidents were reported.
